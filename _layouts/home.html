Financial Times Crosswords (Experiment)

Welcome to our new, experimental crosswords, which can be filled in on-line. We are keen to move on from the old-style, static crosswords, and have high hopes that this new on-line format brings a new (and welcome) possibility to the world of crosswords.

There are, no doubt, some rough edges, broken bits, and "oh dear, we forgot"s, but hopefully there is enough here of interest to encourage you to spend some time, have a play, and then send us some feedback!

N.B. we are particularly keen to hear how these crosswords behave on smartphones.
